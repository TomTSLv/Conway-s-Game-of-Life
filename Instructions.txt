Press p to pause and resume
Press + and - to acclerate and decelerate
Press r to set all cells off
Press 0 to randomly reset
Press f to enable and disable full screen
Press s to save a moment
Press l to load the moment that you saved (only when it is paused)
Press z to make cells bigger
Press x to make cells smaller
Use mouse to toggle on and off cells(only when it is paused)
